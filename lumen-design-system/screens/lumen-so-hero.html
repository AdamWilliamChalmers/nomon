The four signals
Loop
You've accepted several AI outputs in a row without editing or pushing back. Lumen notices — it doesn't judge.
Drift
Over multiple sessions, your active engagement with AI output is trending down. A longer-term pattern.
Mismatch
This conversation conflicts with a goal you've set — like "think independently on strategic questions."
Depth
That's a high-stakes question. Lumen gently asks if you want 30 seconds to form your own answer first.
