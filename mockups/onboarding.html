Deep dive · First run

A calm, one-time invitation — not a wall.

Three quick steps set the foundation (what you use AI for, what to protect, how visible Nomon is), then a short optional tour points out the real controls. Skippable at every step; the extension works regardless.

A — The setup wizard

Everything on by default; opt out, don't opt in

Use the buttons in the card to step through. Presets start selected — the user removes what doesn't fit and adds their own, so a good default is one tap away.

Step 3 of 3 · Visibility

How visible should Nomon be?

You can change this any time from the pill.

Active
Inline cues + reflection cards. The default.
Ambient
Subtle inline cues only — no cards.
Ghost
Nothing in-session; weekly digest only.
Guard
Optional brief hold before sending a prompt that clearly conflicts with a goal. Always bypassable.

Smarter detection, the calibration study and anonymised sharing are on by default — turn any off under Privacy & data in the pill.

Why a wizard, gently. Protected goals are the only user-authored part of Nomon and its biggest differentiator — so setup interrupts once to establish them, but every screen has "Not now", and skipping just leaves a soft pulse on the pill's setup button until done.
