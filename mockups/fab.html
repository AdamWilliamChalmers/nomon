Deep dive · The floating pill

The FAB, in every state — and three ways it could name its jobs.

The mark never changes. What changes is how clearly the pill communicates Mirror, Badge and Cost, and how it reacts to signals, modes and the weekly digest. Hover anything to see it move.

A — The primary interaction

Rest → hover/tap → three-pillar rail

At rest it's just the mark and a quiet wordmark. On hover the wordmark folds away and the three pillars slide out, each with a glyph, a purpose line, and a status dot showing what's on right now.

C — Modes & the weekly nudge

A glance tells you which mode you're in

Ambient is the clean rest pill. Active gets a soft green ring. Guard a firmer purple ring (a goal is live). Ghost fades to dashed. Paused desaturates. The digest-ready dot pulses purple until viewed.
